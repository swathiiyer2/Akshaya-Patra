Volunteer Opportunities

Truck

Our truck drivers are an integral part of our program. They deliver food across 500acres in India from central kitchen to schools as far as in Bangaldesh. Our trucks are built with modern technology to handle tough roads and keep food stable, fresh and hot in transit. Over 2000 pounds of chicken are driven around India in our trucks.

Kitchen

We bake potatoes and rice in our giant factory. Our food is very tasty and goes to feed millions of children in India. Our factory has a well-established process, producing over 10 gallons of masala chai per day, 100 pounds of paneer, and 1000 ounces of chicken tikka masala. Our recipes have been featured in Paula Dean and Top Chef.

Administration

Our administrators do everything for Akshaya Patra. We connect with students from colleges and get help in making websites. We are particularly grateful to the Stanford team for making this really cool page.

Office

Office workers bridge the gap between technology and manwork. They make phone calls and man the front desk, seeking new opportunities with project partners and identifying schools in need of our food.
